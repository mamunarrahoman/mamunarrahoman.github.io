PROJECT TITLE: Electrical Service Design of a floor using AutoCAD considering project requirements.

Project duration: Undergraduate, 3rd Year 1st Semester

Objective: The objective of this project was to design an electrical service drawing based on the parameters and layout of a building floor plan. The project aimed to plan and represent the electrical wiring, load distribution, and placement of electrical components accurately to ensure safe and efficient power distribution.

Tool used: AutoCAD

Figure: AutoCAD layout of electrical service drawing of a floor.

Learnings:

  • Gained hands-on experience in designing electrical service drawings based on floor plan parameters.
  • Learned to plan load distribution, wiring layouts, and placement of electrical components effectively.
  • Improved understanding of electrical safety standards, circuit planning, and practical design constraints.
  • Strengthened skills in interpreting architectural plans and translating them into functional electrical designs.

My role in this project: This was a solo project, and I was responsible for all aspects of the work. I independently analyzed the floor plan, calculated electrical loads, and designed the complete electrical service drawing, ensuring proper layout, component placement, and adherence to safety standards.
